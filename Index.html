Healing toolkit

5 things to do
while you rest 💌

Self-care
Steam + eucalyptus

Run hot water, drop in eucalyptus oil, breathe deep. That blocked nose doesn't stand a chance.

📺
Comfort
Rewatch your comfort show

The one you've seen 10 times already. Blanket on, snacks ready, brain switched off.

🍯
Nutrition
Ginger honey lemon tea

Fresh ginger + lemon + honey + hot water. Steep 5 mins. Tastes exactly like getting better feels.

📖
Slow living
Read something light

A cozy book, a magazine, your fave blog. Nothing heavy — just gentle words while your body heals.

🎵
Mood lift
Build a getting-better playlist

Songs that feel like sunshine. Add one every hour. By the end you'll have your best playlist ever.
